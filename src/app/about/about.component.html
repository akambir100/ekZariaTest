WHO WE ARE

Ek Zaria is an Initiative by We Do Not Accept Money or Things. Its core objective is to act as a bridge between the underprivileged and the donors or volunteers who seek to make a positive impact. Run by the people, for the people. Ek Zaria strives to unite these two ends and bring about a positive change in society.


WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE FOR?

The website serves as a platform where information about the patients in need of medical assistance is provided. All patient profiles listed on the website are verified in person by the NGO to ensure authenticity and transparency. Donors can browse through the patient profiles and select the ones they want to contribute to. This platform helps bridge the gap between patients in need and donors who want to make a difference.
